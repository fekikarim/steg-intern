# Business Goals

Version: 1.0  
Project: STEG Smart Internship & Administrative Workflow Platform

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the business goals of the STEG Smart Internship & Administrative Workflow Platform.

It describes the expected organizational outcomes that justify the development of this solution and explains how the platform supports STEG's digital transformation strategy.

Unlike technical objectives, business goals focus on the **value, impact, and improvements expected at the organizational level**.

---

# 2. Business Vision Alignment

The platform contributes to STEG's objective of modernizing administrative processes through digital transformation.

The solution aims to transform internship management from a fragmented manual process into a structured digital ecosystem based on:

- Process automation.
- Information centralization.
- Workflow standardization.
- Data accessibility.
- Operational efficiency.
- Security and traceability.

The platform represents a first step toward establishing reusable digital administrative services inside STEG.

---

# 3. Primary Business Goals

---

# BG-01 — Digitalize the Internship Management Lifecycle

## Description

Replace traditional paper-based internship management processes with a complete digital workflow covering the entire internship lifecycle.

The platform should support:

- Candidate application.
- Document submission.
- Application review.
- Internship assignment.
- Internship monitoring.
- Evaluation.
- Certificate generation.
- Payment management.
- Archiving.

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Reduction of paper usage.
- Faster administrative processing.
- Better information accessibility.
- Improved process reliability.

---

# BG-02 — Reduce Administrative Workload

## Description

Automate repetitive administrative activities currently performed manually by STEG employees.

The platform should reduce manual operations such as:

- Document preparation.
- Status tracking.
- Reminder sending.
- Approval follow-up.
- Data collection.
- Archive management.

---

## Expected Business Impact

- More efficient HR operations.
- Reduced time spent on repetitive tasks.
- Better employee productivity.
- More focus on high-value activities.

---

# BG-03 — Improve Process Transparency and Traceability

## Description

Provide complete visibility over internship processes through digital tracking and audit mechanisms.

Every important action should be traceable:

- Who performed the action.
- When it was performed.
- What information was modified.
- What decision was taken.

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Better accountability.
- Reduced process ambiguity.
- Easier internal control.
- Improved compliance.

---

# BG-04 — Standardize Internship Procedures

## Description

Create a unified internship management process across STEG departments.

The platform should establish common rules for:

- Application processing.
- Validation workflows.
- Internship assignments.
- Evaluation methods.
- Document management.

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Consistent processes between departments.
- Reduced operational differences.
- Improved quality of internship management.

---

# BG-05 — Improve Collaboration Between Stakeholders

## Description

Facilitate communication and collaboration between:

- Candidates.
- Interns.
- Supervisors.
- HR department.
- Finance department.
- Management.

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Faster information exchange.
- Reduced communication delays.
- Better coordination between teams.
- Improved internship supervision.

---

# BG-06 — Provide Real-Time Management Visibility

## Description

Provide decision makers with reliable information through dashboards and reporting capabilities.

The platform should enable monitoring of:

- Number of internships.
- Internship distribution.
- Department activity.
- Supervisor workload.
- Internship completion rates.
- Historical trends.

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Data-driven decision making.
- Better resource planning.
- Improved strategic management.

---

# BG-07 — Improve Security and Data Governance

## Description

Protect sensitive internship and administrative information through enterprise-level security mechanisms.

The platform should ensure:

- Secure authentication.
- Role-based access control.
- Data confidentiality.
- Audit logging.
- Secure document storage.

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Reduced security risks.
- Better protection of sensitive information.
- Increased trust in digital processes.

---

# BG-08 — Build a Scalable Digital Foundation

## Description

Develop the platform using a modular architecture capable of supporting future STEG digital transformation initiatives.

The solution should not be limited to internship management.

The architecture should support future business domains such as:

- Employee services.
- Training management.
- Recruitment workflows.
- Internal administrative requests.

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Reduced future development costs.
- Reusable technological foundation.
- Long-term digital evolution capability.

# 7. Final Business Goal Statement

The main business objective of the STEG Smart Internship & Administrative Workflow Platform is to establish a secure, efficient, and scalable digital ecosystem that modernizes internship management while creating the foundation for broader administrative digital transformation inside STEG.

The project aims to generate immediate operational improvements while preparing the organization for future intelligent and automated digital services.
